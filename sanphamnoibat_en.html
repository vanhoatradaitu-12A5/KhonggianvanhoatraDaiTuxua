Dai Tu Tea Categories

Discover the 7 signature tea varieties that make the brand of our land.

Click on image to zoom
Ta Tea (Native Tea)
Oldest Original Flavor

The tea variety with the longest history in the area. Large, thin, and lighter leaves than others. Mild fragrance, strong plant, few pests.

Lai 1 Tea (Hybrid)
Most Popular High Yield

The key tea variety in the locality. The tea buds are even, the leaves are thin, soft, and have a very beautiful fresh green color.

Long Van Tea
Exquisite Scent Small Slender Buds

A specialty with small, slender tea buds. A special feature is that it emits a sweet fragrance even while being harvested on the hill.

Bat Tien Tea
Premium Purple-tinted Buds

A precious tea variety with light green leaves and distinct serrated edges. Especially, young buds have a characteristic purple tint.

Kim Tea
Deep Green Color

Small tea buds, tall rising tips. The leaf color is a much deeper green compared to other tea types.

Phuc Tho Tea
Young Rice Aroma

Tea leaves have moderate thickness, releasing a gentle fragrance like new young rice flakes when brewed.

777 Tea Variety
Large & Heavy Buds

A strong tea variety with large buds, heavier weight than others. Thick leaves, yielding a rich flavor.
