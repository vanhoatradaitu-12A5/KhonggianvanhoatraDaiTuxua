General Information

Dai Tu Tea

For a long time, tea has been regarded as a traditional cultural hallmark of the Vietnamese people, vividly reflecting our rustic and simple way of life. From a warm cup of tea shared to welcome guests—symbolizing neighborly bonding—to a reverent bowl of tea offered on ancestral altars.Vietnamese tea culture has resiliently withstood the ups and downs of history to survive and evolve with a unique beauty of its own. It is this very beauty that embellishes the vivid tapestry of our national cultural essence.Embracing this rustic simplicity, Dai Tu proudly stands as one of the regions renowned as the “Tea Capital” of the country. Vietnamese tea culture has breathed soul into the ancient land of Dai Tu to create tea products with a distinctive and unmistakable flavor, to

The fragrance lingers on the way back together.".
